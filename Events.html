BAHASHA YA JIMBO

On 7th December 2023, the diocese of Kigoma held an event known as “BAHASHA YA JIMBO”. The event took place at St maximilian Maria kolbe parish mwilanvya. The function began with the holy sacrifiece of mass led by very Rev Fr Evarist A Guzuye, the vicar General of Kigoma Diosese.

  • Dec 21 2023
  • Fr Albert J Lutumla

PRIEST'S ANNUAL RETREAT 2023

From October 2 to October 6, priests who work in the Diocese had a moment of prayer, meditation and a rest in the time of their retreat which was done at pilgrimage center of Buhabugali, conducted by a Carmelite Priest with other activities, priests had time pass through some topics.
